|ABOUT THE PROJECT

Medical application is growing field in today’s market. The challenge was to develop a medical application for users to get a second opinion from specialists all over India. The application’s UI was created to provide the users an empathetic and emotional connect as soon as they log in. The interactions within the application was designed to provide a smooth flow throughout the application.
