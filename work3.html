|ABOUT THE PROJECT

Experimenting with designing an e-commerce application was the first thing on my mind since I started designing for mobile application. Doing a retail fashion application exposed me into the world of consumer market research for FMCG. Retail mobile application requires the designer to be on par with the current trends in the market so that they can visually communicate to the users.
